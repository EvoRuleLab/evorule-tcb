## 1. Overview

The dual-licensing model is designed to balance two goals:

- **Open source contribution**: a strong copyleft license
  (AGPL-3.0) ensures that improvements to evorule-tcb remain
  available to the community, even when the software is offered
  as a network-accessible service.
- **Commercial sustainability**: a commercial license provides
  a path for organizations whose business model is incompatible
  with AGPL-3.0 to use evorule-tcb, while contributing to the
  financial sustainability of the project.

**Q1. Can I use evorule-tcb internally in my company under

obligation. You must still comply with the other AGPL-3.0
terms (e.g. preserving the license and copyright notices).

Basic technical support is included in the subscription

`COMMERCIAL_LICENSE.md` Section 4 for the full support

**Q3. Can I upgrade from AGPL-3.0 to a Commercial License

Yes. You may at any time enter into a Commercial License

the prior AGPL-3.0 use, provided that use was at all times
in compliance with the AGPL-3.0 terms.

**Q4. Is the Commercial License a perpetual license or a
subscription?**

Both options are available. Perpetual licenses cover the
version of evorule-tcb current at the time of purchase and
do not include updates. Subscriptions include updates and
basic support for the duration of the subscription.
